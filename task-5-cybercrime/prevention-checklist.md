# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Checklist
*For College Students in India*

---

### General Safety

1. **Use strong, unique passwords** – Avoid DOB or "123456". Use letters, numbers & symbols.
2. **Enable 2FA** – On Gmail, Instagram, and all important accounts.
3. **Don't click unknown links** – Verify URLs before opening. Official sites end in `.gov.in` or `.edu.in`.
4. **Avoid public Wi-Fi** for banking, email, or college portals.
5. **Don't overshare on social media** – Keep phone number, address, and Aadhaar off public profiles.
6. **Only install apps from Play Store / App Store** – Never install APKs from WhatsApp links.

---

###  UPI & Financial Safety

7. **Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or CVV** – No bank or app will ever ask for it.
8. **Scanning a QR code sends money, it never receives it** – Ignore anyone asking you to scan to "get paid".

---

###  Report Cybercrime

| | |
|---|---|
|  Portal | [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in) |
| Helpline | **1930** *(call immediately after fraud)* |

> Call **1930 right away** — quick action can freeze the transaction and help recover your money.
