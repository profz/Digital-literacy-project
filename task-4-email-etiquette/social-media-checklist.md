# Social Media — College Student Checklist

## Do's
- Think before you post
- Use privacy settings on all accounts
- Fact-check before sharing anything
- Take screen-time breaks
- Support and uplift your peers online

## Don'ts
- Overshare personal details (address, schedule, finances)
- Post when you're drunk, stressed, or angry
- Engage in cyberbullying or pile-on behavior
- Compare your life to others' highlight reels
- Post anything that violates academic integrity
